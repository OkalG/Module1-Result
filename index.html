Educational And Childhood Background

I was born in Kisii county in a family of 6. It is rumored that I was born after 12 months rather than the conventional 9 months which is still a topic of debate up to today-more like the chicken and egg question. I had the privilege of attending Hannington Academy for elementary school before transferring to Eden Park Academy in Homabay County for junior high. We later moved to Kisii, where I attended St. Benedicts Mixed Academy for my senior year of primary school before transferring to Darajambili Primary School for my final year.

I eventually joined Chavakali Boys' High School, where I graduated in 2021. I am currently a rising freshman at Cornell University (CALS).
